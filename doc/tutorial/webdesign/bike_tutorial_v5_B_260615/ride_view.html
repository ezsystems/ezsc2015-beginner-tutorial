Lyon, France

St-Chamond, France

Description:

I rode with my sister Mary who had never rode a distance like this. We stopped at a nice little Italian Cafe in downtown Schnectady, and after some gellato we headed back along the same route. We stopped at a ball park for a hot dog and watched some baseball, then on to the historic Nyskiunia RR Station building. We stopped again at the Lock 7 park and watched some boats passing through the lock.
