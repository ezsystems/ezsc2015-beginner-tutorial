Geneva, My Favorite Ride

148 Km

Level

Name:

Sarah Haim-Lubczanski

Start:

End:

Lyon, France

Geneva, Switzerland

Description:

I rode with my sister Mary who had never rode a distance like this. We make a refreshing stop at Montalieu-Vecieu, and continued our route. Then we stopped for lunch at a nice little bistro in Hauteville-Lompnes, a very beautiful village. We ate really well, and after some gellato we headed back along the same route. Crossing the border with my sister made things really exciting! You have a totally different perspective when you go places riding your bike. Entering to Switzerland's capital city was really easy. Geneva has really nice bike paths. We stopped at a park for a waffle and watched some soccer, then on to the historic distric we enjoyed shopping chocolates. We'll come back Geneva!

Start! We began our ride from Lyon. It was a very nice ride actually. There were two downhill new tracks just outside the city where I could take advantage of my new bike. It was really awesome. You guys should try this road trail.

Photo: Point of Interest 2

First stop at Montalieu-Vercieu. We need to refill our water bottles. The day was really humid that day. Despite of that, it was a very nice ride actually. We could buy some local cold cuts, really, really good stuff!

Photo: Point of Interest 3

Time for lunch at Hauteville-Lompnes. The ride was very funny so far. Really exciting going through forest and crossing old bridges. this area is really beautiful. Great experience. We were ready for the other half of the ride. Geneva, here we go!

Finish! We made it! We are really proud of ourselves. It was a fantastic ride actually. The entrance to Switzerland capital city is easy, because there are bike lines everywhere. Fantastic! Time for a capuccino and buy some chocolates. We earned them.
